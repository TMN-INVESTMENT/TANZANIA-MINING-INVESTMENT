Frequently Asked Questions

How are daily returns calculated?

Returns are calculated daily: 3% on weekdays (Monday-Friday) and 4% on weekends (Saturday-Sunday). The calculation is based on your investment amount.

Example: TZS 100,000 investment earns TZS 3,000 on weekdays and TZS 4,000 on weekends.

When can I withdraw my profits?

Profit withdrawal availability varies by plan:

  • Copper: Daily withdrawals
  • Gold: Daily or monthly withdrawals
  • Tanzanite: Flexible schedule
  • Diamond: Any time
What is the minimum investment period?

Minimum investment periods:

  • Copper: 7 days minimum
  • Gold: 30 days minimum
  • Tanzanite: 60 days minimum
  • Diamond: 90 days minimum
Can I reinvest my profits?

Yes! All plans support compound interest. You can choose to reinvest your daily profits to grow your investment exponentially. This option is available in your investment settings.

What happens if I withdraw early?

Early withdrawal is possible but may incur a fee:

  • Before minimum period: 10% early withdrawal fee
  • After minimum period: No penalty fees
  • Your earned profits up to withdrawal date will be paid

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our investment platform

How do I create an account?

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" tab on the login page. You'll need to provide:

  • A username
  • Your email address
  • A referral code (required)
  • A secure password
  • Agreement to the Terms and Conditions
Why do I need a referral code to sign up?

The referral code system helps us track how users discover our platform and allows us to reward those who refer new investors. If you don't have a referral code, please contact our support team.

I forgot my password. What should I do?

Click on the "Forgot password?" link on the login page, or contact our support team directly for assistance with account recovery.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept various Tanzanian payment methods including:

  • Vodacom M-Pesa
  • Tigo Pesa
  • Airtel Money
  • Halotel Halopesa
  • CRDB Bank transfers
  • NMB Bank transfers
  • Ezy Pesa
Is there a minimum deposit amount?

Yes, the minimum deposit amount is TZS 1,000.

How long do deposits take to process?

Deposits typically require admin approval before being added to your balance. Once approved by our management team, funds will be available in your account.

What investment plans are available?

We offer four main mineral investment options:

  • Diamonds: TZS 325,000/gram with daily returns
  • Gold: TZS 125,000/gram with daily returns
  • Tanzanite: TZS 275,000/gram with daily returns
  • Copper: TZS 12,500/gram with daily returns
Can I invest in specific minerals?

Yes, our marketplace allows you to invest in various minerals including:

  • Diamonds (TZS 325,000/gram)
  • Gold (TZS 125,000/gram)
  • Tanzanite (TZS 275,000/gram)
  • Copper (TZS 12,500/gram)
How are investment returns calculated?

Returns are calculated based on daily percentage returns specific to each mineral investment. You can track your profits in real-time through the "My Investments" section.

What are the withdrawal limits?

We have the following withdrawal limits:

  • Minimum withdrawal: TZS 10,000
  • Maximum withdrawal: TZS 5,000,000
Is there a withdrawal fee?

Yes, there is a 10% service charge on all withdrawals.

What are the withdrawal processing times?

Withdrawals are processed:

  • Monday-Friday: All day
  • Saturday-Sunday: 2:00 PM - 11:00 PM
How does the referral program work?

When you refer someone using your unique referral code, you earn a commission on their first deposit:

  • 10% commission on deposits between TZS 10,000-500,000
  • 5% commission on deposits between TZS 600,000-5,000,000
How can I share my referral code?

You can share your referral code through:

  • WhatsApp
  • Email
  • SMS
  • Direct link from your dashboard
How secure is my personal and financial information?

We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All transactions are encrypted and monitored for suspicious activity.

Why do I need to verify my account?

Account verification helps prevent fraud and ensures compliance with financial regulations. It also enhances the security of your investment portfolio.

What should I do if I suspect unauthorized activity?

Immediately contact our support team and change your password. We will investigate any suspicious activity on your account.

Still have questions?

Our support team is here to help you with any additional questions you may have.
